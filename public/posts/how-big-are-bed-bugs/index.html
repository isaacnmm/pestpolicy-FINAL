Once the small bed bugs feed on a pet or human blood, they’ll get plumb about double their original size. Further, Bed bug eggs are even smaller - and off-white in color. Bed bug eggs have a tough surface that’ll withstand and survive most insecticides - the reason why you must use steamers (with temperatures over 120 degrees) -, and thus, over 90% of the eggs will hatch successfully.
